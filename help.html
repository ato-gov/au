Learn About Passkeys

What are passkeys?

Passkeys are a secure replacement for passwords using your device's biometrics (fingerprint, face scan, or PIN). They're phishing-resistant and unique per site.

How to set up

  1. Sign in to myGov with your password
  2. Go to Account Settings > Security
  3. Select "Create a passkey"
  4. Follow your device prompts (fingerprint/face/PIN)

Compatible devices

iOS 16+, Android 9+, Windows 11, macOS 13+. Syncs via iCloud Keychain, Google Password Manager, or Windows Hello.

Troubleshooting

Lost device? Sign in with password and create a new passkey. Ensure biometrics are enabled in device settings.
